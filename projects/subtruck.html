Simplify your subscriptions with AI-driven automation

Try Subtruck AI Now

Project Overview

Subtruck AI is a subscription management app developed as part of my Web Development course, designed to simplify the way users handle their subscriptions. Users can input their subscription details, including the service name and renewal date, and Subtruck AI takes care of the rest. Powered by AI, the app automatically finds cancellation links for each subscription, allowing users to cancel with a single click by redirecting them to the appropriate cancellation page.

This project highlights my skills in full-stack development and AI integration. I also developed an iOS app for Subtruck AI, which achieved an impressive #39 ranking on the App Store, showcasing its value to users. Subtruck AI is a practical solution for managing subscriptions efficiently, demonstrating my ability to create user-focused applications with real-world impact.

Skills and Technologies

Project Development

Subtruck AI was built using Laravel, PHP, and Livewire for a robust and dynamic backend, paired with Tailwind CSS for a sleek, responsive frontend. Laravel provided a solid foundation for managing user data and subscriptions, while Livewire enabled real-time updates without page reloads, enhancing the user experience.

The AI component of Subtruck AI was a key focus, automating the process of finding cancellation links for subscriptions. By leveraging AI, the app identifies the subscription service, searches for its cancellation link, and stores it for seamless user access. When a user chooses to cancel, they are redirected to the cancellation page with a single click, streamlining the process.

Additionally, I developed an iOS app for Subtruck AI using Swift, which reached #39 on the App Store. This achievement reflects the app's utility and my ability to deliver a high-quality mobile experience. Subtruck AI showcases my growth in full-stack development, AI integration, and mobile app development.
